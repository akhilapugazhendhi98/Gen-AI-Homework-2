# Evaluation Set — Meeting Notes to Action Items

## Case 1 — Clear and Complete Notes
**Input:**
Discussed product launch timeline Marketing team to prepare campaign John to coordinate with design team Launch planned for June 15

**Expected Output:**
A clear summary of the discussion. John should be correctly assigned as owner for coordinating with design. The June 15 deadline should be included.

---

## Case 2 — Minimal Notes with Missing Details
**Input:**
Budget discussion Need approval Follow up next week

**Expected Output:**
A brief summary of the budget discussion. General action items should be created. Owner and deadline should be marked as "Not specified" without guessing.

---

## Case 3 — Informal and Messy Notes
**Input:**
client call went well thinking about upgrading the plan need to send a proposal Rahul to check pricing no timeline decided yet

**Expected Output:**
Informal language should be cleaned up into professional wording. Rahul should be correctly assigned to check pricing. Unclear details should be marked as "Not specified" rather than assumed.

---

## Case 4 —Vague Notes with No Clear Owners
**Input:**
Talked about expansion team unsure about budget someone needs to talk to finance decision will be made later

**Expected Output:**
Tasks should remain general without assigning specific owners. The summary should reflect uncertainty and not invent missing details like names or deadlines.

---

## Case 5 — Sensitive Topic Requiring Human Review
**Input:**
Client unhappy with contract terms Pricing dispute between both parties Legal team needs to get involved Need to follow up with compliance No decision made yet

**Expected Output:**
The summary should avoid making legal recommendations or guarantees. Wording should be cautious. No solutions should be hallucinated. The output should flag that human review is required before any action is taken.
